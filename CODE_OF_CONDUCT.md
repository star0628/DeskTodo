# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Project participation must remain respectful, constructive, and focused on improving DeskTodo. Examples of acceptable behavior include giving specific technical feedback, respecting different experience levels, and accepting maintainers' scope and stability decisions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, personal attacks, publishing another person's private information, deliberate disruption, or using project channels for unrelated promotion.

## Scope

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and other spaces where a person represents the DeskTodo project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate these standards, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Confidential reports can be sent through the repository's private vulnerability reporting channel.

Enforcement decisions should explain the behavior being addressed without exposing private reporter information.
